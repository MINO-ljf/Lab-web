NSAG: Network Science and Cognitive Intelligence Lab
Network Science & AI Group

Network Science and Cognitive Intelligence Lab is led by Professor Pengfei Jiao and focuses on Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and their wide-ranging applications. The lab is dedicated to advancing research in graph representation learning, heterogeneous graph modeling, dynamic graph analysis, and exploring intelligent algorithms for various domains such as social networks, recommender systems, and bioinformatics. In recent years, the lab has published numerous high-impact papers in top-tier conferences and journals in artificial intelligence and data mining, including NeurIPS, ICLR, KDD, AAAI, and IJCAI, establishing a strong academic reputation.
